About Planet 1 Insurance

Welcome to Planet 1 Insurance, a leading general insurance company founded in 2022 and licensed by the National Insurance Commission to provide motor insurance services in Nigeria. Our operations are fully driven by cutting-edge technology and powered by an innovative and responsive workforce. At Planet 1 Insurance, we have a strong commitment to financial excellence and continuously strive to surpass our previous achievements.

In line with this commitment, we have increased our share capital by 20%, ensuring that we have the necessary resources to meet our customers' needs and provide the best possible services. Our focus on customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do. We understand that financial security is critical to our customers, and we are dedicated to providing the necessary comfort and support when it is needed most.

As part of our commitment to service excellence, we have established partnerships with over 100 motor workshops to ensure that our customers have access to the best support in the event of a loss. With our easy-to-use online platform, we are just a click away, making it easier for our customers to access our services and manage their policies. At Planet 1 Insurance, we are dedicated to providing our customers with the highest level of service and support. Contact us today to experience our exceptional services firsthand.

Our Vision

Delivering innovative and affordable insurance solutions with exceptional customer service, and building a sustainable and profitable company.

Our Mission

To deepen insurance penetration in Nigeria through the use of digital technology, with a focus on ensuring our clients' comfort and peace of mind
